Project Brief :
It is a ticket management system. It us basically used in the organization. In an organization we have different departments 
and each of the department has different projects and those projects has tasks for the employees. 
To organize task we are creating ticket syste. Here you can create tickets of specific department and specific project 
and assign them to employees and once they finish they can close the tickets.
